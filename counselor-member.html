Account Type Reference
TSP (Thrift Savings Plan)
For military / federal employees. Traditional & Roth options. 2025 limit: $23,500 (+$7,500 catch-up ≥50).
Traditional IRA
Pre-tax contributions. Tax-deferred growth. RMDs at 73. 2025 limit: $7,000 (+$1,000 ≥50).
Roth IRA
After-tax contributions. Tax-free growth & withdrawals. 2025 income limit: $150k single / $236k married. Same contribution limits as traditional IRA.
VA Benefits Note
VA disability compensation is non-taxable and does not count as earned income for IRA contribution eligibility. FERS / CSRS pension may reduce SS benefits.
